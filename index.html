In 2005 Gulfport, Mississippi based Hancock Bank and New Orleans based Whitney National Bank – two century-old financial organizations – both served the Gulf Coast region devastated by Hurricane Katrina. Both banks and their employees suffered catastrophic property damage and loss, but Hancock and Whitney each stood strong. In 2011 the two organizations merged and thereafter formed one bank under Hancock Holding Company (Nasdaq: HBHC). Today one of America's strongest, safest, full-service financial institutions, the $21.5 billion company offers comprehensive financial services to clients across a regional market spanning Texas, Louisiana, Mississippi, Alabama and Florida. More at hancockbank.com or whitneybank.com.

Hancock Bank is the trade name used by Whitney Bank in offering banking products and services in MS, AL and FL. Whitney Bank, Member FDIC, is a wholly owned subsidiary of Hancock Holding Company.
